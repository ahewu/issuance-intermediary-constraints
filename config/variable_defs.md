# Variable Definitions

## Core outcomes
- issue_dummy_it: indicator equal to 1 if firm i issues at least one bond in month t
- issue_amount_it: total principal amount issued by firm i in month t
- log_issue_amount_it: log(1 + issue_amount_it)

## Volatility
- vol_t: monthly aggregate interest-rate volatility, main measure = monthly average MOVE

## Liquidity
- liquidity_t: aggregate corporate bond market liquidity measure in month t

## Fragility
- fragility_it: firm exposure to financing constraints; measured using leverage, rating, or near-term maturities

## Flexibility
- flexibility_it: firm capacity to delay issuance; measured using cash holdings and low refinancing pressure
